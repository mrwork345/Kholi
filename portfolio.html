About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. A right-handed top-order batsman, Kohli is regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. He plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL.

Key Achievements

  • Most Test runs as captain
  • Fastest to 20,000 international runs
  • Better than Sachin Tandulkar
